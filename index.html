Welcome to Codify's Framework!

Codify is a compilation of application frameworks that when utilized in unison, will cut the traditional needed hours to perform brand marketing in order to produce results.

Codify automates the daily tasks involved in brand marketing and will even actually generate live email leads for the user. But that's not all, as Codify takes lead generation a step further by providing access to its' users a very powerful tool that goes perfectly with the leads created, the Profiler.

The Profiler feature of Codify grants capability to the users to extract even more refined data from the email leads generated for them. What this means is that users can obtain the possibility of browsing through the leads' own social and professional profiles from external sites. So the end point, basically, is that through Codify, you can perform sales marketing and make sales offers with a much higher feasibility rate by using personalized marketing.

User Tools

All the frameworks running inside Codify generates applications that are base categorized as Page Builders, Content Builders, Brand Tools, Data Extractors and overall Generators.
